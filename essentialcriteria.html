Essential criteria

The following four criteria are essential in the sense that there may be exceptional cases where one of these criteria is not being met, but all the others are, such a basin may still be considered for CO2 storage. However, if more than one of the essential suitability criteria is not being met, then that basin or region should not be considered for CO2 storage.
